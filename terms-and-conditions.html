What Services Does YourSpace Provide?

YourSpace Is Here provides a range of professional mental health and wellness services, including:

  • Online psychological consultations with licensed professionals
  • Self-assessments and emotional wellness tools
  • Wellness workshops and psychoeducational resources
  • Encrypted messaging and secure therapeutic sessions

Our platform connects users across India with qualified psychologists and mental health professionals for evidence-based support designed to improve mental and emotional well-being.

All services are provided by qualified professionals and are intended for non-emergency mental wellness support only.

User Conduct & Safety

YourSpace is a safe, respectful, and professional environment. We are committed to protecting the wellbeing of both our clients and our professionals.

All users are expected to engage with our therapists and staff in a respectful and appropriate manner. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, verbal abuse, or threatening behaviour toward therapists or staff
  • Sexually explicit language, requests, or behaviour of any kind
  • Discriminatory, hateful, or offensive communication
  • Misuse of the platform for purposes other than mental wellness support
  • Sharing false or misleading personal information
We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. Any sexually explicit behaviour or verbal abuse directed toward our therapists will result in immediate termination of the session without a refund. Repeat violations may result in permanent account suspension.
🚨

Is YourSpace Suitable for Emergencies?

No. YourSpace Is Here is not an emergency service and is not equipped to handle medical crises or psychiatric emergencies.

Our services are designed exclusively for non-emergency mental health and wellness support. If you are experiencing any of the following, please seek immediate help:

  • Suicidal thoughts or self-harm ideation
  • A psychiatric emergency or mental health crisis
  • A medical emergency of any kind
  • Immediate risk to yourself or others
If you are in immediate danger, please call 112 (Emergency Services) or visit your nearest hospital. You may also reach the iCall helpline at 9152987821 for mental health crisis support.

Fees and Refunds

All consultation and session fees must be paid in advance prior to the commencement of any session. Bookings are considered confirmed only upon receipt of payment.

Rescheduling: We understand that plans can change. We offer flexible rescheduling options with adequate notice (minimum 24 hours prior to the session).

Refunds: Refunds for missed, cancelled, or late-cancelled appointments are granted solely at the company's discretion. This policy is in place to protect the time and professional commitments of our therapists.

  • Cancellations with 24+ hours' notice may be eligible for rescheduling
  • No-shows or same-day cancellations are not eligible for refunds
  • Refund decisions are final and made on a case-by-case basis
  • Sessions terminated due to user misconduct will not be refunded
For queries about payments or refunds, please contact us at help@yourspaceishere.in
